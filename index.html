Spa and Wellness Center

Inspired by the ancient healing traditions of the East and the science of the West, Conscious Path is a Wellness Spa dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health for the mind, body, and soul. We have two locations for Conscious Path: Marrakech, Morocco and Istanbul, Turkey. We offer a wide variety of ancient healing modalities from China, India, and beyond. We also offer the latest in Skin Care techniques from Western Science making our Spa truly a holistic experience. Our spa is focused on using only the finest organic ingredigents in all our products. Our slow-food restaurant sources local, fresh, organic fare and herbs from nearby farms and merchants, making dining with us truly exceptional.

We offer custom Spa Packages that are hand-tailored towards your specific needs. Please contact our staff and we can advise you on the best treatments for you.
